📖 How to fill in this report

1. Actual hours worked
Enter the real start and end times for each person who worked this shift. Your start time is pre-filled 15 minutes earlier than the shift start (manager setup time). Adjust any times if someone arrived late, left early, or stayed longer.

2. End-of-day inventory
Count and record what's left over at the end of the shift:
• Strawberries — note how many flats or packs remain (e.g. "2 flats", "half a flat")
• Anko — note how many bags or containers remain (e.g. "1 bag", "about half")

3. Memo for next shift
Leave any notes the next manager should know — equipment issues, low supplies, anything unusual that happened, or tasks left undone.

4. Submit
Hit Submit report when done. The owner will review and approve it. You can update and resubmit any time before it's approved.
